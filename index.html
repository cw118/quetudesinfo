WHY CEGEP?

  • Internationally recognized for high-quality education
  • A close-knit and stimulating learning environment for students
  • Interactive and dynamic courses with a focus on student success
  • Be part of a vibrant student life and community
  • Increased accessibility to higher education
  • Explore your interests, passions, and career options
  • Stepping stone to university and/or the workforce
  • A CEGEP diploma is in increasing demand on the job market
Sources: Fédération des cégeps, EducationUSA Canada, SRAM
