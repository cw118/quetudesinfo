Important Dates

A condensed calendar of important events and deadlines for CEGEP applicants, parents, and curious students. For links to official CEGEP sites and (virtual) open house sites, see Important Links (opens in a new tab).

Student-for-a-Day visits can usually be scheduled at individual CEGEPs from October to February (time period/months vary for each college). Visit the official sites for details and to register.

As of January 17, application modules for Marianopolis, SRAM (Vanier, John Abbott, Heritage, Champlain Lennoxville), Dawson, and Champlain St-Lambert are open (accepting applications). Remember that it is not first-come first-serve, and that the deadline to apply is March 1!

Fall 2022 Deadline

High school students and more: the 2022 Fall semester application deadline is Tuesday, March 1, 2022. CEGEP application modules will open in mid-January. See below (or SRAM's website) for SRAM round 2 and 3 deadlines.

SRAM Round 2 Deadline

If you weren't accepted in SRAM's first round, you can apply to round 2 (round 1 decisions are/will be posted on April 2) before April 21, 11:59 PM. See the table of available programs for SRAM-affiliated CEGEPs here.

SRAM Round 3 Deadline

If you weren't accepted in SRAM's first or second rounds, you can apply to round 3 (round 2 decisions are/will be posted on May 11) before May 26, 11:59 PM. See the table of available programs for SRAM-affiliated CEGEPs here.
